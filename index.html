about me

Ms. Oberman is a student majoring in Interior Design at Texas State University. She has obtained a 3.8 GPA leading her to membership in the Phi Kappa Phi honor society.

Ms. Oberman has obtained extensive experience in Revit, InDesign and Photoshop. When she isn’t glued to the computer screen, you can find her at the local coffee shop trying every drink, taking a nice walk around the park, or trying to catch up on her favorite Netflix series. She is currently working on a commercial project for a youth shelter called “The Harbor House.”
